# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 24, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the UMP Inspector browser extension. UMP Inspector operates entirely locally on your device and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or browsing information.

## 2. Data Accessed

To function correctly and provide its core diagnostic features, UMP Inspector requires access to the following categories of data:

* **Website content:** The extension accesses and inspects network requests and responses specifically on YouTube to capture UMP payload data.
* **User activity:** The extension monitors local network traffic by intercepting the `fetch` API to isolate, extract, and display these specific requests during your active session.

## 3. Data Processing & Storage

All data extraction, interception, and inspection occur entirely locally. Absolutely no network data, or personal browsing information is transmitted to external servers.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing

Because no data is transmitted off your device, no user data is sold, transferred, or shared with any third parties, analytics providers, or advertisers.
